"Barcelona will reportedly receive a whopping €500 million for Camp Nou’s title rights over a 20 year deal with Spotify once all the renovations are done. According to SPORT, Barcelona are only a few formalities away from signing a global sponsorship deal with the audio streaming platform Spotify. While this includes changing the shirt sponsor, the team is also working on selling Camp Nou’s naming rights during the remodelling of the stadium. As reported before, the first team kit sponsorship deal is valued at €60 million while the part on the back at €5 million. Furthermore, once the Espai Barça work concludes, the team will look for a new contract that will be worth around €20-25 million. Meanwhile, the initial agreement signed between the club and Spotify would consist of €280 million for the next three seasons in total, and it is suggested that the team expects a majority of this amount to invest in the possible signing of Erling Haaland in the summer.
